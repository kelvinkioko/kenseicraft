3. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users of Kensei Craft. All data generated by the application, such as business details, client information, and invoices, is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any user information, we do not use your data for any purposes. The data you input into the application remains on your device and is under your control.

5. Data Storage

We do not store any of your personal data remotely. All data is stored locally on your device. You are responsible for managing and backing up your data.

6. Legal Basis for Processing Your Data

Since we do not process any user data, the legal bases outlined under the GDPR are not applicable to our operations.

7. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal data with any third parties.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the application against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, since all data is stored locally on your device, we recommend you follow best practices for device security.

9. Data Retention

We do not retain your personal data. All data is managed and retained by you on your device.

10. Your Data Protection Rights

Under the GDPR, you have certain rights regarding your personal data. Since we do not collect or process any user data, these rights are not applicable in the context of our application. However, you maintain full control over the data you input into the app.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
